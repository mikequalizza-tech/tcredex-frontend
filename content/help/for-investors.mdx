## Overview

tCredex connects investors with high-quality tax credit opportunities across LIHTC, HTC, OZ, and other programs.

This guide covers everything you need to know as an investor using the platform.

---

## Your Investor Dashboard

When you log in, your dashboard shows:

- **Pipeline** - Deals you're actively evaluating
- **Matches** - New opportunities that fit your criteria
- **Commitments** - Active investment commitments
- **Closing Room** - Due diligence workspaces

---

## Setting Your Investment Criteria

Your investor profile determines which opportunities AutoMatch surfaces.

### Investment Parameters
- Minimum/maximum investment size
- Target credit types (LIHTC, HTC, NMTC, OZ, State)
- Yield targets
- CRA focus areas

### Geographic Preferences
- Target states and markets
- Assessment area alignment (for CRA purposes)
- Metro vs. non-metro preferences

### Deal Preferences
- Preferred sectors
- Risk tolerance
- Timeline requirements

> **Tip:** Align your profile with your current investment strategy. Update as priorities shift.

---

## Finding Opportunities

### AutoMatch

AutoMatch analyzes new deals against your investment criteria and notifies you when opportunities align.

Match factors include:
- Credit type and structure
- Geographic fit (especially CRA alignment)
- Deal size and timing
- Project quality and readiness

### Marketplace Browse

Browse the Marketplace directly to discover opportunities outside your typical parameters.

Filter by:
- Credit program
- State/region
- Investment size
- Deal stage

---

## Evaluating a Deal

### Key Information on DealCards

- **Project Overview** - Type, location, sponsor track record
- **Credit Structure** - Program, allocation, pricing
- **Financials** - Capital stack, sources and uses
- **Impact Metrics** - Jobs, community benefit, CRA eligibility
- **Timeline** - Construction start, projected close
- **Documents** - Due diligence materials

### CRA Considerations

For bank investors, DealCards highlight:
- Assessment area alignment
- Community development purpose
- Qualified investment characteristics

---

## Expressing Interest

When you find a deal worth pursuing:

1. Click **Express Interest** to notify the sponsor/CDE
2. Request additional due diligence materials
3. Move to formal commitment discussions

---

## The Closing Room

Once parties agree to proceed:

- **Document Repository** - All due diligence materials in one place
- **Checklist Tracking** - Monitor outstanding items
- **Communication** - Direct messaging with deal parties
- **Commitment Management** - Track terms and conditions

---

## Commitment Process

Formal commitments include:

- Investment amount
- Credit pricing
- Key conditions
- Timeline to close

All parties sign off before moving to final closing.

---

## Portfolio View

Track your full investment portfolio:

- Active commitments
- Closed deals
- Credit delivery schedules
- Compliance monitoring
