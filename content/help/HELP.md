# tCredex Platform User Guide

## Welcome to tCredex

tCredex is a comprehensive tax credit marketplace platform that connects project sponsors, Community Development Entities (CDEs), and investors to facilitate tax credit financing for community development projects.

### What is tCredex?

tCredex streamlines the tax credit investment process by providing:

- **Automated Deal Scoring**: Our proprietary 4-pillar scoring system evaluates projects based on community distress, impact potential, project readiness, and mission alignment.
- **Intelligent Matching**: Advanced algorithms match projects with the most suitable CDEs and investors.
- **Transparent Workflows**: Clear, auditable processes for Letters of Intent (LOIs), commitments, and closing.
- **Comprehensive Tracking**: End-to-end deal management from intake to compliance.

### Supported Tax Credit Programs

tCredex supports multiple federal and state tax credit programs:

- **NMTC** (New Markets Tax Credit)
- **HTC/LIHTC** (Housing Tax Credit/Low-Income Housing Tax Credit)
- **OZ** (Opportunity Zone)
- **Brownfield** (Brownfield Tax Credit)
- **State-specific programs** (NMTC, HTC variants)

## User Roles and Permissions

tCredex operates with four primary user roles, each with specific permissions and access levels.

### Sponsors (Project Developers)

Sponsors are typically real estate developers, non-profit organizations, or government entities seeking tax credit financing for their projects.

**Key Permissions:**
- Create and manage project deals
- Access the deal intake form
- View deal scoring and matching results
- Receive and respond to LOIs from CDEs
- Participate in closing rooms
- Track deal progress through all stages

**Dashboard Access:**
- Deal creation and editing
- Project portfolio overview
- Communication with matched CDEs and investors

### Community Development Entities (CDEs)

CDEs are organizations that receive tax credit allocations from the CDFI Fund and deploy them through qualified projects.

**Key Permissions:**
- Browse the public deal marketplace
- View detailed deal information and scoring
- Issue Letters of Intent (LOIs) for suitable projects
- Manage allocation tracking
- Participate in deal closing processes

**Dashboard Access:**
- Allocation management dashboard
- LOI tracking and management
- Deal pipeline overview
- Geographic focus area management

### Investors

Investors are typically banks, financial institutions, or investment funds seeking tax credit investments to meet CRA requirements or generate returns.

**Key Permissions:**
- Browse public deals in the marketplace
- View deal details and financial projections
- Issue commitment letters for suitable investments
- Track investment pipeline
- Participate in closing processes

**Dashboard Access:**
- Investment criteria management
- Commitment tracking
- Deal pipeline monitoring
- Portfolio performance analytics

### Administrators

Platform administrators have full access to system management and oversight functions.

**Key Permissions:**
- Full access to all deals and user data
- System configuration and maintenance
- User management and support
- Platform analytics and reporting

## Getting Started

### Account Registration

1. **Navigate to the Platform**
   - Go to the tCredex platform URL
   - Click "Sign Up" or "Create Account"

2. **Select Your Organization Type**
   - Choose from: Sponsor, CDE, Investor, or Admin
   - Provide your organization details

3. **Complete Registration**
   - Enter your personal information
   - Set up authentication credentials
   - Verify your email address

4. **Profile Setup**
   - Complete your organization profile
   - Set preferences and criteria (for CDEs/Investors)
   - Upload necessary documentation

### First Login

After registration, you'll be directed to your role-specific dashboard. Take a moment to:

- Review your profile information
- Set up notification preferences
- Familiarize yourself with the navigation
- Complete any required onboarding steps

## Deal Workflow Overview

Understanding the deal lifecycle is crucial for effective platform use. Deals progress through several stages:

### 1. Draft Stage
- Sponsor creates initial deal entry
- Basic project information is captured
- Deal remains private until submitted

### 2. Intake Stage
- Comprehensive project details collected
- Document uploads and verification
- Initial eligibility screening

### 3. Matched Stage
- Deal enters public marketplace
- Scoring engine evaluates project
- Matched with suitable CDEs and investors

### 4. Term Sheet Stage
- Interested parties submit LOIs/commitments
- Negotiation and due diligence begins
- Terms are formalized

### 5. Commitment Stage
- Final commitments secured
- Legal documentation prepared
- Closing preparations begin

### 6. Closing Room Stage
- Document vault activated
- All parties coordinate closing
- Final approvals and funding

### 7. Closed Stage
- Transaction completed
- Tax credits issued
- Compliance monitoring begins

### 8. Compliance Stage
- Ongoing compliance tracking
- Annual reporting
- Tax credit recapture monitoring

## For Sponsors: Creating and Managing Deals

### Starting a New Deal

1. **Access Deal Creation**
   - From your dashboard, click "Create New Deal"
   - Or navigate to `/intake`

2. **Complete the Intake Form**
   - **Basic Information**: Project name, location, program type
   - **Financial Details**: Total cost, tax credit request, equity structure
   - **Project Details**: Description, timeline, impact metrics
   - **Eligibility**: Census tract data, compliance requirements

3. **Upload Documentation**
   - Project plans and specifications
   - Financial projections
   - Environmental assessments
   - Legal documents

4. **Submit for Review**
   - Review all information for accuracy
   - Submit deal for scoring and matching

### Managing Active Deals

- **View Deal Status**: Track progress through workflow stages
- **Respond to Inquiries**: Handle information requests from interested parties
- **Manage Communications**: Use platform messaging for deal discussions
- **Update Information**: Modify deal details as needed

### Working with LOIs and Commitments

- **Review LOIs**: Evaluate offers from CDEs
- **Accept/Reject Offers**: Make decisions on partnership proposals
- **Negotiate Terms**: Use platform tools for term sheet development
- **Track Commitments**: Monitor investor interest and commitments

## For CDEs: Finding and Pursuing Deals

### Browsing the Marketplace

1. **Access Marketplace**

2. **Filter and Search**

   - Specify allocation amount and terms

3. **Submit LOI**

- **Track LOIs**: Monitor status of submitted LOIs
- **Allocation Management**: Track available and committed allocations
- **Deal Pipeline**: View all deals where you've expressed interest

## Scoring and Matching System

### Understanding Deal Scoring

tCredex uses a proprietary 4-pillar scoring system:

1. **Distress (40%)**: Community need and economic indicators
2. **Impact (35%)**: Potential community benefit and job creation
3. **Readiness (15%)**: Project preparedness and feasibility
4. **Mission Fit (10%)**: Alignment with CDE/investor mission

### Scoring Tiers

- **Tier 1 - Greenlight**: High-priority projects (80+ points)
- **Tier 2 - Watchlist**: Moderate priority (60-79 points)
- **Tier 3 - Defer**: Lower priority (0-59 points)

### Matching Algorithm

- Matches deals with CDEs based on:
  - Geographic service areas
  - Allocation availability
  - Program preferences
  - Mission alignment

- Matches deals with investors based on:
  - Investment criteria
  - CRA requirements
  - Yield expectations
  - Program preferences

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: What tax credit programs does tCredex support?**
A: tCredex supports NMTC, HTC/LIHTC, Opportunity Zones, Brownfield credits, and various state programs.

**Q: How does the scoring system work?**
A: Our 4-pillar system evaluates distress, impact, readiness, and mission fit to prioritize projects.

**Q: Is the platform secure?**
A: Yes, we use enterprise-grade security with encrypted data, RLS policies, and audit trails.

### For Sponsors

**Q: How long does deal intake take?**
A: Initial intake typically takes 30-60 minutes, depending on project complexity.

**Q: When will my deal be visible to CDEs/Investors?**
A: Deals become visible after scoring and entering the "matched" stage.

**Q: Can I edit my deal after submission?**
A: Yes, you can update deal information until commitments are finalized.

### For CDEs

**Q: How do I track my allocations?**
A: Use the CDE dashboard to monitor available and committed allocations by year and program.

**Q: Can I set geographic preferences?**
A: Yes, configure your service areas in your profile settings.

**Q: What happens after I submit an LOI?**
A: The sponsor receives notification and can accept, reject, or negotiate terms.

### For Investors

**Q: How do I set investment criteria?**
A: Configure preferences in your investor profile for deal size, programs, and geographic focus.

**Q: Can I see other investors' commitments?**
A: No, commitments are private between investors and sponsors.

**Q: How are deals prioritized for me?**
A: Deals are matched based on your specified criteria and preferences.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

**Q: Can I access tCredex on mobile devices?**
A: Yes, the platform is responsive and works on tablets and phones.

**Q: Is there an API available?**
A: API access is available for enterprise clients; contact support for details.
