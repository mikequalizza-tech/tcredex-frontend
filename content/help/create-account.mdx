## Before You Start

tCredex supports four organization types. Choose the one that fits your role:

| Type | You Are... |
|------|------------|
| **Sponsor** | Developer, nonprofit, or community org seeking financing |
| **CDE** | Community Development Entity with NMTC allocation |
| **Investor** | Bank, insurance company, or fund purchasing credits |
| **Admin** | Platform administrator (by invitation only) |

Not sure? Most users are **Sponsors**. If you're submitting a project for financing, that's you.

---

## Step 1: Go to Sign Up

Visit [tcredex.com/signup](/signup) or click **Get Started** from the homepage.

---

## Step 2: Enter Your Information

### Personal Details

- **Email** - Your business email (this becomes your login)
- **Password** - Minimum 8 characters, include numbers and symbols
- **First Name** - Your first name
- **Last Name** - Your last name

### Organization Type

Select your organization type:

- **Sponsor** - Submit projects, find CDEs and Investors
- **CDE** - Deploy allocation, find qualified projects
- **Investor** - Purchase credits, find opportunities

> **Important:** You cannot change organization type after signup. Choose carefully.

---

## Step 3: Organization Setup

After basic signup, you'll set up your organization:

### For New Organizations

- **Organization Name** - Your company or nonprofit name
- **Website** - Your organization's website (optional)
- **Address** - Business address
- **Logo** - Upload your logo (optional)

### Joining an Existing Organization

If your organization already has a tCredex account:

1. Ask your admin to send an invitation
2. Click the link in the invitation email
3. Complete your personal profile

---

## Step 4: Complete Your Profile

Different organization types have different profile requirements:

### Sponsors

- Company information
- Contact details
- (Optional) Past project experience

### CDEs

- Certification number
- Parent organization
- Allocation details
- Geographic focus
- Sector preferences

### Investors

- Institution type (bank, insurance, fund)
- Investment criteria
- Target credit types
- Geographic preferences
- CRA assessment areas (if applicable)

---

## Step 5: Verification

Some actions require verification:

| Action | Verification Needed |
|--------|---------------------|
| Browse Marketplace | None |
| Submit Project | Email verified |
| Issue LOI | Organization verified |
| Issue Commitment | Organization verified |

### Email Verification

- Check your inbox for verification email
- Click the link to verify
- Check spam if you don't see it

### Organization Verification

- CDEs: Provide CDFI Fund certification number
- Investors: May require accreditation documentation
- Sponsors: Standard verification based on profile

---

## After Signup: What's Next?

### If You're a Sponsor

1. Go to [Submit Project](/intake) to start your first project
2. Complete the Intake Form to generate your DealCard
3. Reach Tier 1 to appear on the Marketplace

### If You're a CDE

1. Complete your CDE Profile with allocation details
2. Set your deployment preferences
3. Start receiving matched projects

### If You're an Investor

1. Complete your Investor Profile with criteria
2. Set investment parameters
3. Browse opportunities or wait for matches

## Troubleshooting

### Didn't receive verification email?

- Check spam/junk folder
- Add support@tcredex.com to contacts
- Request new verification email from Settings

### Picked wrong organization type?

- Contact support@tcredex.com
- We can help you set up a new account

### Organization already exists?

- Ask your organization's admin for an invitation
- Don't create a duplicate organization
