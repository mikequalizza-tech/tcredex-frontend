## Overview

tCredex is designed to make tax credit financing accessible, transparent, and fast.  
Most features are completely free, and additional premium tools may be introduced as the platform expands.

Below is a breakdown of what’s available today and what’s coming soon.

---

## Free Features

All users get access to the following at no cost:

### **Project Intake Form**

Submit your project details, determine eligibility, and generate your DealCard.

### **DealCard Publishing**

Once you reach Tier 1 (40% completion), your project becomes visible on the Marketplace.

### **AutoMatch**

Receive automated matches with CDEs, investors, and credit buyers based on:

- Census tract
- Program eligibility
- Project type
- Financing needs
- Community impact

### **Marketplace Access**

Browse active projects, view public DealCards, and explore tax credit programs.

### **Document Uploads**

Upload Phase I reports, site plans, financials, and more.  
Files are auto‑tagged for easy organization.

### **Closing Room (Basic)**

Move matched deals into a shared workspace with:

- Document checklists
- Messaging
- Status tracking

Premium tools are optional and designed for users who want deeper insights or enhanced visibility.

### **Priority Matching**

- Allocation trends

### **Enhanced Closing Room**

Includes:

- Expanded document storage
- Advanced collaboration tools

### **Portfolio Management**

For repeat developers, CDEs, and investors managing multiple projects.

Most users start with the free plan and upgrade only if they need:

- Faster matching
- More visibility
- Advanced analytics
- Team collaboration tools

The platform is designed so you can scale at your own pace.
