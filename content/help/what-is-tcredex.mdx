## Overview

tCredex is the AI-powered marketplace for tax credit financing.  
It connects developers, nonprofits, CDEs, investors, and credit buyers through a streamlined platform designed to make complex transactions faster, clearer, and more accessible.

Whether you're working on a community facility, housing development, historic rehab, or mixed-use project, tCredex helps you understand your eligibility, structure your deal, and connect with the right partners.

---

## Why tCredex Exists

Tax credit financing is powerful but notoriously complex.  
Developers often struggle with:

- Understanding program eligibility
- Finding the right CDE or investor
- Navigating compliance requirements
- Managing documents and due diligence
- Coordinating multiple stakeholders

tCredex was built to solve these challenges by providing a single, unified system for the entire process.

---

## Key Features

### **Project Intake Form**

A guided workflow that helps you enter your project details, determine eligibility, and generate your DealCard.

### **DealCard**

A clean, standardized project profile that CDEs and investors can quickly evaluate.

### **AutoMatch**

AI-driven matching that identifies the best partners based on:

- Census tract
- Program eligibility
- Project type
- Financing needs
- Community impact

### **Marketplace**

A transparent space where projects and capital providers can discover each other.

### **Closing Room**

A secure workspace for due diligence, document sharing, and deal coordination.
